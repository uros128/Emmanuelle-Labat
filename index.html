About Me

I am a hardworking, caring and motivated professional with three years of experience to help patients affected by brain injuries in different units (intensive care, neurosurgery department, rehabilitation unit).

With ambition and hard work, I made a protocol based on the last neurosciences recommendations to help TBI patients and their families in an intensive care unit. Even if I can work in total autonomy, I believe that the team spirit is the key to offer the best care for the patients.
